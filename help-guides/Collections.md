# Collections

Collections help users assemble customized sets of records that share a common context, such as a network area, service, or project. Each collection can contain multiple object types, which are displayed in a structured tabular view. Within the Collections Workspace, items are grouped by type (for example, all nodes appear together, all connectors together, and so on). Each group can be expanded or collapsed for easier navigation and organization.

They are typically created and removed from the **Network Explorer Workspace**, but their detailed contents are maintained within the **Collections Workspace**.

---

## Managing Collections

- **Creating and Removing Collections:**  
  Collections are created or deleted through the **Network Explorer Workspace**. Once created, they appear in the Collections tree under their respective collection type.

- **Opening Collections:**  
  Double-click a collection in the Explorer tree to open it in the **Collections Workspace**. The workspace presents all items in a clear, grouped format.

- **Adding Items:**  
  Drag and drop objects (e.g., nodes, connectors, or paths) directly from the **Network Explorer Workspace** into the open collection. The item appears immediately in the grid under the appropriate group.

- **Removing Items:**  
  Select one or more items in the grid, right-click, and choose **Delete** from the context menu. The item is removed from the collection immediately.

- **Saving Changes:**  
  Click **Save** on the toolbar to confirm modifications. Deleted records are fully removed (not struck through) once saved.
